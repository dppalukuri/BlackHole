## Path A: Data Product Options

### Data 1: India Company Intelligence API

- **Source:** MCA company data, GST verification, SEBI filings
- **Enrichment:** AI risk scoring, director network mapping, compliance status
- **Price:** $49-149/mo
- **Competition:** Zauba Corp (dominant but no modern API), Tofler (limited), Probe42 (enterprise)
- **Revenue ceiling:** $5K-20K/mo

### Data 2: WARN Act Layoff Tracker (US)

- **Source:** 50 state WARN databases (all public, free)
- **Enrichment:** Company matching, trend analysis, geographic heatmaps
- **Price:** $29-99/mo
- **Competition:** WARN Firehose ($49/mo, basic), Revelio Labs (enterprise-only)
- **Revenue ceiling:** $2K-10K/mo
- **Why first:** Easiest to build. MVP in days. Proven demand.

### Data 3: UAE Real Estate Intelligence API

- **Source:** Dubai Land Department open data (free CSV/API), listing sites
- **Enrichment:** ROI predictions, rental yield, area comparisons, investor alerts
- **Price:** $49-199/mo
- **Competition:** REIDIN (expensive), DXBiQ (free beta), Property Monitor (acquired)
- **Revenue ceiling:** $3K-15K/mo

### Data 4: India Trade/Import-Export Intelligence

- **Source:** DGFT trade statistics, port-level customs data
- **Enrichment:** AI supplier matching, trend alerts
- **Price:** $29-79/mo (vs ImportGenius $229/mo, Export Genius $278/mo)
- **Competition:** ImportGenius, Export Genius, Volza (all charge 3-10x more)
- **Revenue ceiling:** $3K-15K/mo
