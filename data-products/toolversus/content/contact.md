## How to reach us

The fastest way to contact ToolVersus is by **email: palukuri.biz@gmail.com**

We read every message and aim to reply within 2–3 business days. For urgent corrections (e.g., misleading pricing, broken affiliate link, factual error), we usually respond same-day.

## What to email about

### Corrections

Spotted a price that's gone up since we last verified, an audit date that's wrong, or a feature claim that no longer matches the product? Tell us. Please include:

- The URL of the page where you saw it
- The specific claim that's incorrect
- A source where the correct information lives (the merchant's pricing page, an audit report, etc.)

We fix verifiable errors quickly and add a "corrected on [date]" footnote to the page. We don't accept "I disagree with the verdict" as a correction request — verdicts are editorial judgements; if you want us to reconsider one, send the case for the alternative ranking and we'll evaluate it on the next quarterly review.

### Partnership inquiries

If you're a SaaS company that wants to be **included in a comparison** we don't yet cover (or thinks our existing comparison should add a tier or sub-page), email us. We'll evaluate based on:

- Whether the category fits our existing scope (VPN, hosting, email marketing, password managers, AI tools)
- Whether your product has independent reviews / audits we can use as evidence
- Whether the category has enough buying-decision difficulty to warrant a comparison

We do **not** accept payment for inclusion in comparisons or for higher rankings. We do accept standard affiliate partnerships through public networks (Impact, CJ, ShareASale) — being an affiliate partner does not change a product's ranking, and is disclosed on every page.

### Feedback / suggestions

Found a comparison helpful? A specific axis we should be testing differently? A category we should cover next? We genuinely want this feedback. ToolVersus is a small site; what readers actually find useful shapes what we build next.

### Press / interviews

For media inquiries (especially around our methodology, the affiliate-disclosure framework, or the bias-audit process we run on our own content), email with the publication name and your deadline.

### Bug Reports

Email us with steps to reproduce — the page URL, what you did, what you expected to happen, and what actually happened. Screenshots help. We typically fix obvious bugs (broken affiliate link, broken table on mobile, factual error in a comparison) within 48 hours.

## What we don't do

- **No paid product placement.** Don't email us asking to "feature" your product for a fee — we don't accept these.
- **No SEO link-exchange schemes.** Don't email us about "guest posts" with embedded backlinks for a payment.
- **No fake reviews or testimonials.** We don't write reviews of products we haven't researched, even when paid.
- **No hidden affiliate insertions.** If you want us to add an affiliate link to your product, apply through your standard affiliate network and email us — we'll evaluate it for fit, and disclose it like any other.
