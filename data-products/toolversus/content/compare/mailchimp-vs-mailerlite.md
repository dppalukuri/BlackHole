## The Quick Answer

**MailerLite wins for most people.** It's half the price, has a cleaner interface, includes a website builder and paid newsletters on all plans, and has significantly higher user satisfaction (G2: 4.7 vs Mailchimp's 4.3). Mailchimp's advantage is its massive integration ecosystem (300+ vs 140) and deeper analytics for e-commerce.

## Pricing — The Biggest Difference

| Contacts | Mailchimp (Standard) | MailerLite (Growing Business) | Savings |
|----------|---------------------|------------------------------|---------|
| 500 | $20/mo | $10/mo | **50% less** |
| 1,000 | $30/mo | $15/mo | **50% less** |
| 5,000 | $75/mo | $39/mo | **48% less** |
| 10,000 | $110/mo | $73/mo | **34% less** |
| 50,000 | $385/mo | $289/mo | **25% less** |

MailerLite is consistently 25-50% cheaper at every tier. Over a year at 5,000 contacts, that's **$432 saved** — enough to invest in content, ads, or tools.

**Free plan comparison:** Mailchimp gives 500 contacts with 1,000 sends/month. MailerLite gives 1,000 contacts with 12,000 sends/month. MailerLite's free plan is 2x more generous.

## Features That MailerLite Includes for Free

MailerLite bundles features that Mailchimp charges extra for:

- **Website builder** — included on all plans (Mailchimp has this too, but MailerLite's is better)
- **Paid newsletters** — monetize with subscriber-only content (Mailchimp doesn't offer this)
- **Blog** — built-in blog feature (Mailchimp doesn't have this)
- **Unlimited emails** — on all paid plans (Mailchimp limits sends by tier)

## Where Mailchimp Still Wins

- **Integrations:** 300+ native integrations vs MailerLite's ~140. If your stack includes niche tools, Mailchimp is more likely to connect
- **E-commerce analytics:** Revenue tracking, purchase behavior, product recommendations — deeper than MailerLite for Shopify/WooCommerce stores
- **Brand recognition:** Some enterprise clients expect to see "Powered by Mailchimp" — it signals credibility
- **Advanced reporting:** Campaign comparisons, audience insights, predictive demographics
