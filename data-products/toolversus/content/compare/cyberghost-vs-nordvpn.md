## Privacy & audits

Both have no-logs policies. Audit frequency and rigor differ:

**NordVPN:**
- Audited by **Deloitte (2022, 2023)** and **PwC (2020)**.
- Based in **Panama** — no data retention laws.
- RAM-only server infrastructure (fully rolled out 2023).
- Owned by Nord Security — which operates Mullvad independently (separate companies, different ownership).

**CyberGhost:**
- Audited by **Deloitte (2024)** — their first major no-logs audit.
- Based in **Romania** — EU member, but outside 14-Eyes direct participation.
- Maintains "NoSpy" servers in their Romanian HQ as an extra layer.
- Owned by **Kape Technologies**, which also owns **ExpressVPN** and **Private Internet Access** — consolidation concerns for privacy purists (Kape was previously called "Crossrider" and had adware history in 2013-2016).

Kape's ownership of multiple major VPNs is a real concern for some users. If corporate structure matters to you, NordVPN (Nord Security, owns only itself + Mullvad which operates independently) has a cleaner story.

**Edge: NordVPN** on audit track and ownership transparency.

## FAQ

**Is CyberGhost safe to use?**
Yes. Despite Kape's adware past (as Crossrider in 2013-2016), CyberGhost's modern infrastructure is legitimate — independently audited by Deloitte (2024), uses RAM-only servers, keeps no logs. The "Kape owns both ExpressVPN and CyberGhost" concern is a corporate-structure one, not a technical-safety one.

**Why is CyberGhost so much cheaper?**
Different market strategy. CyberGhost targets mass-market budget-shoppers with aggressive 2-year discounts. NordVPN positions slightly higher and invests more in speed + audits. Both are sustainable businesses.

**Does CyberGhost work in China?**
Inconsistently. You can connect sometimes, but it's not reliable enough to depend on for work. NordVPN's obfuscated servers are more consistent, and ExpressVPN is still the gold standard for China reliability.

**Does NordVPN offer a free trial?**
No direct free trial, but a 30-day money-back guarantee that's genuinely honoured. Install, use for up to 30 days, request a refund — confirmed no-hassle in multiple independent reviews. CyberGhost offers 45 days.

**Can I stack CyberGhost + NordVPN for more privacy?**
Not recommended — VPN-over-VPN typically breaks both kill switches and halves speed. Use NordVPN's built-in Double VPN instead if you want multi-hop.
