## The Quick Answer

**NordVPN wins.** It's nearly half the price of ExpressVPN ($3.39/mo vs $6.67/mo on annual plans), faster in speed tests, has twice as many servers, and includes extras like a built-in ad blocker and malware protection that ExpressVPN doesn't offer.

ExpressVPN's only real advantages are: (1) it works more reliably in China, and (2) its app is slightly easier to use. For 99% of people, NordVPN is the better deal.

## Pricing Comparison

This is where NordVPN pulls ahead decisively:

| Plan | NordVPN | ExpressVPN |
|------|---------|------------|
| Monthly | $12.99 | $12.95 |
| 1-Year | ~$4.59/mo | $6.67/mo |
| 2-Year | **$3.39/mo** | Not available |

NordVPN's 2-year plan is nearly **50% cheaper** than ExpressVPN's annual plan. Over 2 years, that's a savings of roughly $79. Both offer 30-day money-back guarantees.

## Speed

NordVPN's proprietary NordLynx protocol (built on WireGuard) consistently outperforms ExpressVPN's Lightway protocol in independent speed tests. On a 500 Mbps connection:

- **NordVPN**: 400-450 Mbps typical (nearby servers)
- **ExpressVPN**: 300-380 Mbps typical (nearby servers)

Both are fast enough for 4K streaming and gaming. The difference matters more if you have a gigabit connection.

## Privacy & Security

Both VPNs have been independently audited for their no-logs policies:

- **NordVPN**: Audited by Deloitte (2022, 2023). Based in Panama (no data retention laws).
- **ExpressVPN**: Audited by KPMG and Cure53. Based in British Virgin Islands. Uses RAM-only servers (TrustedServer).

ExpressVPN's RAM-only servers are a genuine security advantage — data is physically impossible to retain across reboots. NordVPN has started implementing this but hasn't fully rolled it out.

**Edge: ExpressVPN** on pure privacy architecture, but both are excellent.

## Streaming

Both unblock Netflix, Disney+, Hulu, BBC iPlayer, and Amazon Prime Video reliably. NordVPN has a slight edge with more servers to fall back on if one gets blocked.

For streaming in the UAE (where VoIP and some content is restricted), both work well. NordVPN's obfuscated servers are specifically designed for restricted networks.

## Who Should Choose ExpressVPN?

- You live in or travel to **China** frequently (ExpressVPN is more reliable behind the Great Firewall)
- You want the **absolute simplest** VPN app with zero learning curve
- You're an **Apple ecosystem** user (ExpressVPN's iOS/macOS apps are best-in-class)
- Privacy architecture matters to you (RAM-only servers)

## Who Should Choose NordVPN?

- You want the **best value** for money
- Speed is important (gaming, 4K streaming, large downloads)
- You want **extra features** (ad blocker, malware protection, Dark Web monitor)
- You need a **dedicated IP** address
- You have many devices (10 connections vs ExpressVPN's 8)
