## The Quick Answer

**Hostinger wins.** It's the fastest-growing host in the world (29M+ users) for good reason — better speeds, lower pricing, more data centers, and higher satisfaction scores. Bluehost rides on its WordPress.org endorsement, but Hostinger's LiteSpeed servers outperform Bluehost's Apache setup in every speed test.

## Pricing Comparison

| Plan tier | Hostinger | Bluehost |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| Entry | $1.99/mo (Premium, 100 sites) | $1.99/mo (Basic, 10 sites) |
| Mid | $2.99/mo (Business, 100 sites) | $4.99/mo (Choice Plus, unlimited) |
| Renewal | ~$7.99/mo | ~$12.99/mo |

Same entry price, but **Hostinger gives you 100 websites** where Bluehost only gives 10. At renewal, Hostinger is 38% cheaper. Over a 3-year term, Hostinger saves you approximately $180.

## Speed

Hostinger uses **LiteSpeed web servers** (the fastest web server technology available). Bluehost uses Apache (older, slower). In independent benchmarks:

- **Hostinger:** ~1.2 second average page load, 99.9% uptime
- **Bluehost:** ~1.8 second average page load, 99.9% uptime

That 0.6 second difference matters — Google uses page speed as a ranking factor, and every 100ms of latency costs ~1% in conversion rate.

## The WordPress.org Endorsement

Bluehost has been "officially recommended by WordPress.org" since 2005. This is their biggest marketing asset. But here's what most people don't know:

- WordPress.org recommendations are **affiliate partnerships** — WordPress.org earns revenue from Bluehost referrals
- Hostinger is equally optimized for WordPress with 1-click install, staging, and WP-CLI
- In actual WordPress performance benchmarks, Hostinger's LiteSpeed + LiteSpeed Cache plugin outperforms Bluehost

The endorsement means Bluehost works well with WordPress. It doesn't mean it's the fastest or best value.

## Where Bluehost Wins

- **Phone support:** 24/7 phone support on all plans (Hostinger is chat-only)
- **WordPress.org endorsement:** Credibility signal for some users
- **US-focused:** If you only need US hosting, Bluehost's 2 US data centers are fine
