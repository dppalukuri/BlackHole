FAQ

My domain is on GoDaddy. Do I have to host there too?
No. You can keep your domain on GoDaddy and point it to Hostinger's servers. Just update the nameservers or A record in your GoDaddy DNS settings. Takes 5 minutes and saves you significantly on hosting costs. Many people use GoDaddy for domains and a separate host for better performance.
Is GoDaddy's website builder better than Hostinger's?
GoDaddy's builder is simpler (fewer options, harder to mess up). Hostinger's AI-powered builder is more flexible and produces better-looking sites. For complete beginners who want zero decisions, GoDaddy's builder works. For anyone willing to spend 30 minutes learning, Hostinger's is better.
