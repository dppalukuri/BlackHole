The Quick Answer

Surfshark wins. At $2.19/mo vs ExpressVPN’s $6.67/mo, Surfshark costs roughly one-third the price while offering comparable speeds, better device support (unlimited vs 8), and a built-in ad blocker that ExpressVPN lacks.

ExpressVPN’s only defensible advantages are its superior app design and reliability in China. For everyone else, paying 3x more for ExpressVPN is hard to justify.

Speed

ExpressVPN is marginally faster:

  • ExpressVPN: 300-380 Mbps (Lightway protocol)
  • Surfshark: 300-400 Mbps (WireGuard protocol)

The difference is minimal and varies by server. Both handle 4K streaming, gaming, and video calls without noticeable lag. Speed should not be the deciding factor here.

The Unlimited Devices Advantage

This is Surfshark’s killer feature. Unlimited simultaneous connections means one $2.19/mo subscription covers:

  • Your phone, laptop, tablet
  • Your partner’s devices
  • Kids’ devices
  • Smart TVs, gaming consoles
  • Router (covers everything on your network)

ExpressVPN limits you to 8 devices. A family of 3-4 people can easily exceed this.

Where ExpressVPN Justifies the Premium

1. China and restricted countries: ExpressVPN is the most reliable VPN for use inside China, Iran, and other countries with heavy internet censorship. If you travel to or live in these countries, ExpressVPN is worth the price.

2. App simplicity: ExpressVPN’s apps are genuinely the easiest to use. One-tap connect, minimal settings, beautiful design. If you’re setting up a VPN for non-technical family members, ExpressVPN causes fewer support calls.

3. TrustedServer (RAM-only): All ExpressVPN servers run entirely in RAM — no hard drives. This means server data is physically wiped on every reboot. Surfshark has partially implemented this but hasn’t achieved full coverage.

FAQ

Is Surfshark safe despite being cheaper?
Yes. Price doesn't determine security quality. Surfshark has been independently audited by Deloitte for its no-logs policy, uses the same WireGuard encryption as premium VPNs, and has never had a verified security breach. Lower pricing is possible because of Surfshark's lean operating model and aggressive customer acquisition strategy.
Which is better for UAE residents?
Both work in the UAE. Surfshark offers better value since VPN use in the UAE is primarily for VoIP calls (WhatsApp, FaceTime) and accessing geo-restricted content — tasks where both VPNs perform equally well. Save the $100+ difference and invest it.
Can I switch from ExpressVPN to Surfshark?
Yes, at any time. Sign up for Surfshark (30-day money-back guarantee), test it for a week, and if satisfied, cancel your ExpressVPN subscription. Both are month-to-month after the initial term.
