## THE MENTAL MODEL

You are not a freelancer. You are building a **value extraction machine**.

Every action falls into one of four categories:

1. **HARVEST** - Get money now (freelancing, selling)
2. **BUILD** - Create assets that generate money later (products, content, code)
3. **AUTOMATE** - Remove yourself from the process (delegation, scripts, systems)
4. **COMPOUND** - Make existing assets generate more (reinvest, optimize, scale)

In Week 1, you're 80% HARVEST, 20% BUILD.
By Week 4, you're 20% HARVEST, 30% BUILD, 30% AUTOMATE, 20% COMPOUND.

The rat race is 100% HARVEST forever. That's what you're escaping.

## CRITICAL RULES

1. **Ship ugly, improve later.** Your first product, first proposal, first website - ship it TODAY. Polish later.
2. **Respond in < 1 hour.** Speed of response wins clients in UAE. WhatsApp = instant.
3. **Overdeliver on first projects.** First 3 clients = your foundation. Give them 150%.
4. **Get testimonials.** After EVERY project, ask for a LinkedIn recommendation + written review.
5. **Raise prices weekly.** Start at $45/hr, raise to $75/hr by week 2, $100+ by month 2.
6. **Track everything.** Use the CRM in this engine. What's measured improves.
7. **No perfectionism.** Done > perfect. Always.
8. **Stack skills publicly.** Everything you learn, share as content. Content = compounding lead gen.
9. **Think in systems.** "How do I never do this manually again?" after every task.
10. **Protect your energy.** Sleep 7+ hours. This is a marathon designed as a sprint.
